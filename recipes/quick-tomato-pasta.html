Quick tomato pasta

Weekday sauce is a fresh quick tomato sauce from scratch that you can make easily any night of the week. Taken from NOT ANOTHER COOKING SHOW

Ingredients

  • San Marzano tomatoes
  • Pasta- linguine preferred
  • Basil
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes
  2. Thinly slice garlic
  3. Add a good amount of olive oil to pan
  4. Add garlic and basil to cold oil to allow them to infuse as oil reaches temperature
  5. Gently heat pan until oil is bubbling
  6. Add blended tomatoes, with a pinch of salt
  7. Turn up heat and cook hard and fast for 15 minutes, making sure to keep the sauce moving
  8. After 15 minutes, begin cooking your pasta
  9. Add a generous handful of parmesan to the sauce
  10. Simmer sauce on a low heat until pasta is al dente
  11. Add pasta into sauce, with more parmesan if you like
  12. Plate and enjoy (finish with even more parmesan and a sprig of basil)
